Healthy diets

Diets play a vital role in our overall health and wellness. A healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables and low fat dairy can help reduce cholesterol and high blood pressure reducing the risk of getting heart diseases. A balanced diet contains an adequate amount of nutrition which is important for our body to grow properly and be disease-free.

A balanced Diet

Carbohydrates

Portion of food

Even if you are eating a healthy food but in huge amount then your body needs then it may cause you a weight gain. So taking a measurement or controlled portion in every meal you are having will help you with maintaining your health and weight.

Hydration

60 % of the human body in men and 55 % in women is made of water. So, it differs with age, gender, and weight of the person to know how much water to take in a day. Around 2 lr of water will be enough to hydrate an adult human. Consulting nutrition will be the best idea to know how much fluid intake is good for your body to hydrate throughout the day if you are into any professional competition to maintain your physic.

Whole food VS processed

it will be good for every human to take serious care of their health while having whole or unprocessed foods, lean proteins, fresh fruits, and vegetables are better choices.
